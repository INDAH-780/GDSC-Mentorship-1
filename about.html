GET TO KNOW ME

About MYFIRM

At MYFIRM, our lawyers belief that an inform client is bette able to make effective decisions. Many of our clients come to us facing the most difficult time in their lives. Our experience has shown us that divorce, custody battles, sexual harassment, employment discrimination and injury cases should be handled to minimize stress and cost. We take the time to fully educate our clients about the legal process and develop a strategy tailored to our clients' goals and objectives.

Practice Areas

We represent people in many civil matters, with a focus of our practice on divorce and family law, sexual harassment and employment discrimination, and many other areas of litigation.

Experienced Lawyers

attorneys have handled a number of high-profile cases for individuals and businesses. We use our many years of experience to help our clients resolve disputes in matters related to divorce and family law, employment, contracts, probate, personal injuries, and more

At our law firm, we understand the toll that a long, drawn-out legal proceeding can take on you and on your family. With this in mind, we always work hard to negotiate a creative solution whenever possible. Our goal is to reduce stress and contentiousness while often saving you money in the process. However, we also realize that an amicable resolution is not always realistic. When needed, we are prepared to utilize our knowledge and advocacy skills in litigating your case in the courtroom.

Our Qualifications

Over the past 33 years, our team has refined their skills and developed our firm into the successful law practice it is today. All of our firm's attorneys have trial experience in both state and federal courts in Indiana. When it comes to acting as a neutral mediator, our attorneys have the experience, knowledge, and professional credibility to convince otherwise obstinate parties to realistically assess the merits of their case and come to a resolution. Our attorneys possess the strong negotiating skills, trial experience, and knowledge of the appellate process to guide your case to the finish line.
