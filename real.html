Because real estate matters typically involve significant sums of money, it is important to protect your financial and legal interests by hiring an experienced attorney. At the Law Office of James F. White, P.C., we are experienced in handling both residential and commercial real estate matters. We understand the intricacies of real estate law and know how to protect our clients throughout any property transactions.

Experienced Residential Real Estate Counsel

When buying or selling a home, a good attorney can minimize the risk involved and ensure a smooth transaction. Our attorney is skilled in drafting and reviewing residential real estate contracts that are beneficial to and protective of our clients.

We are experienced in working with all elements of residential real estate contracts including disclosures, mortgage contingencies, home inspection contingencies, and home sale contingencies. Whether we represent buyers, sellers, landlords or tenants, we work closely with our clients to ensure they understand their options and responsibilities and are protected at every step. Our goal is to provide high-quality, affordable legal representation in all residential real estate transactions.
